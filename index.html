How we get paid

Three ways to partner

Same outbound engine, three ways to structure the deal. We'll recommend the one that fits your business — you decide on the call.

Model 01

Commission per deal

One-time, paid when a deal closes

The simplest version. We book the meetings, you close, and you pay a one-time commission on each closed deal. Nothing before, nothing after.

Best forOne-off or high-ticket sales, project work, clean books
You payA set % or flat fee per closed deal
We carryThe outbound cost until a deal lands
Model 02 · most popular

Revenue-share partner

Ongoing % of the revenue we generate

No retainer, no upfront. We take a share of the revenue from what we source — for as long as it keeps paying. We earn as you earn, so we're built to keep the pipeline full.

Best forRecurring or repeat-revenue businesses wanting aligned incentives
You payA share of sourced revenue, or an agreed line
We carrySetup, infrastructure, and the risk of a slow start
Model 03

Equity partner

A stake in the business we help grow

The deepest version. We build and run your outbound growth engine in exchange for equity — real skin in the game, fully aligned for the long haul.

Best forFounders building something big who want a true growth partner
You giveAn agreed equity stake, vesting over time
We carryThe channel, long-term, as owners
